RLx-Core

A zero-toy, plug-and-play RL pipeline I’m carving out for real automation — not demo videos.

Why RLx-Core exists

I’m tired of “RL in robotics” papers that line‑up a MuJoCo Hopper, claim novelty, and call it a day. Factories, warehouses, and outdoor robots need controllers that survive drifting joints, busted sensors, part tolerances, and operators who kick the cell at 3 a.m. RLx‑Core is my long‑haul proof that modern RL can out‑perform PID/MPC/H₂/H∞ once the problem stops being clean and academic.
